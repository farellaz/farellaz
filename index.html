About Me

Political Science graduate from Universitas Airlangga (GPA 3.59) with professional experience in legal administration at Bawaslu East Java. I specialize in electoral dispute resolution, managing high-stakes documentation for the Constitutional Court, and inter-agency coordination. My technical expertise encompasses legal analysis, Microsoft Office, supported with good English proficiency and multimedia editing skills in Canva, CapCut, and Adobe Premiere. I am a meticulous team leader with a focus on analytical problem-solving.

During my tenure at the General Election Supervisory Agency of East Java, I was entrusted with reviewing electoral complaints, preparing travel accountability reports, drafting legal memos, and collaborating with stakeholders such as the Regional Police and Prosecutor’s Office through the Integrated Law Enforcement Center (Sentra Gakkumdu).

Actively involved in leadership and administrative roles within student organizations, I have coordinated multi-departmental events, managed professional development programs, and strengthened partnerships through effective communication and project management.

I am particularly interested in government relations, public policy analysis, and political consulting. I aspire to contribute to initiatives that connect institutional governance with real-world impact, driven by critical thinking, collaboration, and solution-oriented research.
